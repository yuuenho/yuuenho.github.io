About Me
I think it's really easy to spot bad design, those are often the ones you have the most frustrating experiences with. Good design however is sometimes invisible, it's seamless and often goes unnoticed. Creating for me has always been an opportunity to learn.

My love for storytelling came from playing video games as a kid. While I was reading lots of world mythology and was entrenched with fairytales. I found a passion for videos with deep and compelling storylines. Stories drove me to want to play more. It made me curious about why the characters were the way that they were and how the story will unfold. Hands down favorite game would be Final Fantasy 6.

Over the years, I started making costumes. I love where that's taken me. From sewing costumes traditionally, it's gotten me into prop making, where with really unconventional tools like soldering iron and LEDs. One of my first costumes I made and wore to Center Island during Victoria Day weekend, unravelled on me.
